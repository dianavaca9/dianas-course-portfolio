TOP 5 LIST OF FAVORITE INDIAN FOODS

  1. Butter Chicken

    Butter chicken is a popular indian dish, made of seared chicken with rich indian spices such as: koori methi, garam masala, ginger garlic, and chili powder, cooked in a creamy tomato based sauce, served with rice or garlic naan bread. Butter chicken is sweeter than tikka masala.

  2. Chicken Tandoori

    Chicken tandoori is a popular indian dish, marinated in yogurt and spices such as: garam masala, cumin, turmeric, chili powder, garlic and ginger, and it is cooked in a traditional clay oven called a "tandoor" where naan bread is also cooked.

  3. Chicken Tikka Masala

    Chicken tikka masala is a popular indian dish, made using chicken tighs (for better tatse) marinated in yogurt, and all the traditional indian spices such as: garam masala, cumin, turmeric, chili powder, garlic and ginger etc. Tikka masala is less creamier, and more spicier than butter chicken.

  4. Veggie Biryani

    Veggie biryani is a popular indian dish, made with basmati rice, mixed vegetables such as: carrots, peas, potatoes, cauliflower, and traditional indian spices such as: garam masala, cumin, turmeric, and saffron. It is often garnished with fried onions and fresh cilantro, and served with raita (yogurt sauce).

  5. Pani Puri

    Pani puri is a popular indian street food, made of hollow, crispy puris (fried dough balls) filled with a mixture of spicy tamarind water (pani), chickpeas, potatoes, and spices (filling).
